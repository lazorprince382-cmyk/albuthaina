Frequently asked questions

For current project availability, transfer instructions or partnership arrangements, confirm directly through the official WhatsApp number.

01

What work does Al Buthaina support?

Programme areas include clean water, food and Ramadan Iftar, Qurbani and Aqeeqah, orphan and family relief, medical assistance, clothing, shelter, mosque support and Quran distribution.

02

Where does the organisation work?

Al Buthaina is based in Mbale, Uganda. Field activities are arranged around verified community needs and available support.

03

How can I donate?

The website displays client-supplied Stanbic Bank details. Confirm the account, currency, branch, SWIFT information and any alternative transfer instructions through the official WhatsApp number before sending money.

04

Does this website process payments?

No. The website does not accept card payments or store transfer information. Donations are arranged outside the website using confirmed instructions.

05

Can I volunteer or propose a partnership?

Yes. Contact the organisation with your name, location, relevant skills or organisation, and the kind of programme you want to support. Opportunities depend on current needs.

06

Can supporters request field information?

Supporters and prospective partners can ask the organisation for appropriate project updates, photographs, video and delivery information through the official contact channel.
