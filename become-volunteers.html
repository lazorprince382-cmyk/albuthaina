Before you join

What we expect

01

Respect and dignity

Treat community members with courtesy and never promise assistance that has not been approved.

02

Responsible media use

Follow local guidance before photographing or recording adults, children or vulnerable people.

03

Safe, reliable service

Follow field instructions, protect confidential information and communicate honestly about your availability and skills.

Interested in helping?

Send your name, location, relevant skills and the kind of work you are interested in through the official WhatsApp number. Do not send identity documents until the organisation confirms they are required.
