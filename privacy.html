Website information

Privacy and responsible media use

This page explains how this informational website handles visitor information and how to contact Al Buthaina Relief Charity Organisation about published material.

Information collected by this website

This website does not create visitor accounts and does not provide an online contact or payment form. It does not currently use advertising cookies or website analytics. The hosting provider may process standard technical logs, such as IP address, browser type and requested pages, for security and reliable delivery.

WhatsApp and external services

Contact and donation-confirmation links open WhatsApp, which is operated by a third party under its own privacy terms. Do not send sensitive identity or financial documents until you have confirmed that you are communicating with the organisation’s official representative.

Donations

The website displays client-supplied bank information but does not process payments or store card or bank-transfer details. Confirm the account and any missing transfer information through the official contact number before sending funds.

Photographs and videos

Field photographs and videos are published to document community needs and programme activity. The organisation is responsible for obtaining appropriate permission and protecting the dignity and safety of identifiable people, especially children and vulnerable individuals.

Corrections and removal requests

If you are shown in published material, are responsible for a child shown, or believe information is inaccurate, contact the organisation to request review, correction or removal.
