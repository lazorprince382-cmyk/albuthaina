Our Philosophy
Our philosophy is rooted in Islamic principles of compassion (Rahmah), accountability (Amanah), and justice (Adl). We believe that helping others is a fundamental obligation, and we work to empower communities through sustainable development while maintaining transparency and integrity in all our operations.
Our Organization
Al Buthaina Relief Charity Organisation serves communities through clean-water projects, food and orphan support, Qurbani and Aqeeqah, wheelchair distribution, school and mosque support, Ramadan programmes and Quran distribution. We are committed to responsible, community-focused service.
Our Impact
Our field activities respond to practical community needs. Supporters can follow photographs and videos from water, food and seasonal-relief work, while every donation and volunteer contribution helps strengthen the next response.
